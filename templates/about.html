About This NGS Analysis Platform

Welcome to NGS Analysis by Babukanthsamy - your one-stop solution for streamlined, reproducible, and customizable Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) workflows.

Why This Platform?
  • Run your own NGS pipelines with ease, directly from your browser.
  • Choose from a curated set of industry-standard tools and custom scripts.
  • Track progress, view interactive reports, and download results securely.
  • Designed for both beginners and experienced bioinformaticians.
Key Features
  • FASTQ quality control, trimming, and filtering
  • Read alignment and variant calling
  • Interactive quality reports and visualizations
  • Batch processing and workflow automation
  • Secure file management
