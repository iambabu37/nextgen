Help & FAQ

Get the most out of your NGS analyses. If you have questions, you’ll likely find answers below!

How do I upload my sequencing data?

Go to the Upload page, select your FASTQ file(s), and click "Upload". You’ll see a progress bar indicating upload status.

What tools and workflows are available?

This platform supports popular NGS tools such as FastQC, Trimmomatic, BWA, GATK, and more. You can select specific tools or run a full workflow.

How do I view and download my results?

After processing, results will be displayed on the results page. Interactive reports can be viewed in your browser, and all output files are available for download.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data is stored securely and only accessible to you. Files are automatically deleted after a set retention period.

Need more help?

Please contact support for further assistance.
